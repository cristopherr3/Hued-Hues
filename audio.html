Warmth Hued Excitement

Welcome, to Warm Hue Hues, a fan page, perhaps, pages; that are for the oddly, but extraordinary character of Warmth Hue Excitement! Yes. Weird, but welcome to the amazing world of Warmth!

The environment sounds like subtle train noises (passing by, tracks screeching quietly in the distance), snow gently coming down as an elegant pound, and an ambience of white noise, along with a dream-like hum to the urban landscape. All the noises are heard, as an endless winter scenery, although that isn’t the character entirely, there’s a light in the background to where “they” (me) huddles by for warmth. Listening, in a constant state of patience, listening to R&B,“oldies”, and soft rap.
